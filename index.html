Follow these steps to becoming a student on "the Hill!"

Submit your Confirmation Deposit

Say "Yes" to joining the best college community in the South with your enrollment deposit. As a resident student, your deposit of $350 reserves residential housing space for you and a place in the class, as well as, ensures you will receive summer orientation information. A commuter deposit of $200 reserves a place for you in the class and ensures you will receive summer orientation information.

Make your deposit online today

Spring Hill College uses the afford.com online payment system.

Schedule a Campus Visit

If you haven't already, you need to schedule your visit to the Hill! Our 380-acre campus is one of the most picturesque you will find. You can choose a Senior Select Visit Day or a private visit. Dates for the Visit Days are available on the calendar to the right.

Schedule a visit now

Log in to BadgerWeb

BadgerWeb is our information hub for students, faculty, and staff. Once you have made your deposit, it will become your primary source for information about Spring Hill. You will receive a username and password when your BadgerWeb account has been created.

Log in to BadgerWeb

Submit Your Test Scores

Be sure to send any additional ACT or SAT scores you receive. With an increase in score, we will look to see if you are eligible for any additional scholarships.

Complete the FAFSA

Once you enter the Spring of your Senior Year, you need to file the FAFSA. The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) will help our Financial Aid Office determine if you are eligible for any additional funding to help make Spring Hill College more affordable for you and your family.

Submit your FAFSA online
